TRANSFORMING ASIA NORTH AMERICA

TANA is the umbrella grouping through which John Rogers organizes his independent, yet interrelated, professional activities.

As a long-time attorney and former in-house General Counsel, Rogers works and teaches at the intersection of law and business especially in the international arena. His skills, acquired over 30 years of legal practice, enhance seminar and speaking engagements and bring real world experience to both the boardroom and the class room.

Rogers’ advise is often sought by companies seeking to execute on international opportunities and understand operations, particularly on issues of corporate governance, finance, and business ethics.

He currently resides in Beijing.
